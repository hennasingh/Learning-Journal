My frontend developer bootcamp journey.

I love to write my learnings and share it with the community. I started with Frontend Developer Career Path from scrimba on Aug 22, 2023 and I have penned down my learnings since. At the end of Oct, I made a big move to join the bootcamp, to get more expert reviews for my projects and meet like-minded peers.

How I stay committed to learning

Commitment is not easy. It requires discipline and consistency. It also has to be backed by a desire to achieve. It was initially difficult to set a routine but I am now determined to switch to development and share my knowledge via writing or speaking with the world.

How I got started

Writing has always been my passion. I believe I can explain concepts to others very clearly. Writing what I am learning also helps me reflect on what I learned and refer them back anytime I need to look at a concept.
